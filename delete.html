Account Deletion

Delete Your Lux Verbi Data

You can delete your data directly inside the Lux Verbi app.

Steps

  1. Open Lux Verbi
  2. Go to Settings
  3. Select Data & account
  4. Tap Delete my data
  5. Confirm deletion

What Gets Deleted

  • Onboarding answers
  • Preferences
  • Saved verses and prayers
  • Server records tied to your device ID

Subscription Notice

Deleting your data does not cancel subscriptions. Manage subscriptions in your App Store or Google Play account.
